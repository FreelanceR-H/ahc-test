ABOUT US

Make It Great Again!

Alesayiah Holding was initially established and developed by Sheikh Ali Abdullah Alesayi in 1968. Our key business segments are Manufacturing, Services, Trading, and Investment. We contribute to national and regional economic growth and follow strategic thinking and long-term planning; therefore, we have proliferated and became one of the largest and leading trading corporate in Saudi Arabia and the GCC region. AHC is keen to positively contribute to its surroundings and community through healthy relationships that encouraged constant improvement.

OUR HERITAGE

When the company was founded in 1968, it was known as Ali Abdullah Alesayi Establishment, Sheikh Ali decided to combine all the companies of his establishment into a newly formed entity named 'Alesayiah Holding.' In line with the economic boom in Saudi Arabia, Sheikh Ali desired to support the Saudi Arabia and GCC region industry, to persist in inventing and adapting to cope with the increasing technological era, to broaden across Saudi Arabia and the GCC Region through constant work and maintain the integrity of our business. During a few years, Alesayiah Holding managed to turn their family business into a strong, regionally respected, and confident handler of the massive distribution network of some famous and well-known regional and international brands through our trading divisions, which now operates in Saudi Arabia and the GCC region, to enrich everyday products and services that connect us to history, art, culture, identity, and cohesion for communities and in our great civilizations.
